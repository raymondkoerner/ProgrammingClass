San Diego Zoo

The Sandiego zoo is probably the most known thing in Sandiego and in my opoinion the best thing in Sandiego. The Sandiego zoo has a ton of animals and is famouse for it. And did you also know that the Sandiego zoo is one of the oldest zoos in the world.

Sea World

Sea world in Sandiego is just like the zoo there. It has a ton of sea animals like otter, crabs, and a ton of different species of fish. It is mostly known and famouse for the killer whale they have at the exibit.

USS Midway Museum

Seventeen years ago, the USS Midway Museum opened its doors to the public. Since 2004, more than 16 million guests, military members and school students have been educated, inspired and entertained exploring the hallowed decks of America’s Living Symbol of Freedom. It took the imagination, hard work and passion of a group of unrelenting visionaries to transform Midway from a Navy warship into the world’s premiere floating naval ship museum.

La Jolla Cove

La Jolla cove is a very amazing place in Sandiego. It is a small beach. It is very rich in marine life and is protected by the government. La jolla cove is open to the public and surrouned by cliffs. It is loved by both locals and visitors from around the world. From its breathtaking sunsets and majestic sea lions to seaside restaurants and kids activities, this beautiful setting offers a slice of the elegant yet laid back La Jolla lifestyle for all to enjoy.

Balboa Park

Balboa Park is a 1,200-acre historic urban cultural park in San Diego. And it contains a Museum and 7 theaaters there and it is near the Sandiego zoo. It is atcually walking distance from the Sandiego zoo.

Sunset cliffs Natural park is a great place to go in Sandiego. It is very nice. And is right near the beach.
